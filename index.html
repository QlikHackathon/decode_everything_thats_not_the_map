How are you helping #saveourocean?

The United Nations (UN) has held a conference and multiple events to support the Implementation of Sustainable Development Goal 14 (SDG14): Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas and marine resources for sustainable development, aims to be the game changer that will reverse the decline in the health of our ocean for people, planet and prosperity. The voluntary commitments are solutions-focused with engagement from all, including Member States and the broader community

This site allows you to explore the UN Member States, broader community and oceans to see who has volunteered to meet the various commitments for SDG14 and see what those specific commitments are. When defined, commitments have been encouraged to follow the SMART Criteria - a commitment should be:

  • Specific
  • Measurable
  • Achievable
  • Resource based, with
  • Time-based deliverables
We hope that this interactive experience will help raise awareness on the importance of the Ocean and the implementation of SDG14.
